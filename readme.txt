BUAS Intake Assignment "Bounce" by Zhangir Nurmukhambetov

How to Play the Game: (Note: all keys activate on release)
A - Left
D - Right

J - Turn into balloon
K - Turn into dodgeball
L - Turn into rock

Z - Switch theme (black/white)

Goal: Reach the "WIN!" level

Reach the flag to complete the level.
Avoid touching skulls. They will kill you.
Accelerators speed you up into a certain direction.
Avoid getting crushed by moving tiles. They will kill you.

There are 10 levels (excluding the "WIN!" level). First 7 are "tutorial" levels.
The last 3 are custom maps. They might require more skill to complete.

Enjoy.
